创新的重要性

​ 假设人类社会想要获得并享受长时间的繁荣，对人类来说维持创新的思维是必要的。

​ 生活在信息时代，我们使用大量的基于创意的科技产品，例如数码相机，笔记本电脑以及智能手机。这些创意为我们带来了极大的方便，激发了我们对于未来生活的热情，并且推动我们的社会向前发展。事实上，有许多智者已经意识到创新对我们社会发展的重要性：没有创新，产业不可能现代化；没有创新，我们的医学不可能治愈如此多的致命疾病；没有创新，就不可能又我们今天使用的电子产品。

​ 我们应该牢记，创新的确促进了社会、科技、文化的发展。因此，学校里的学生应该被教育批判性思考从而培育他们的创新意识；科学家们应当获得经济上的支持来产生创意并推动创新研究。当然，作为大学生，我认为学生非常有必要提出创新方法来提高学习效率。

Supporting【假设】 that human society wants to acquire【获得】 and enjoy long-time boom, it is necessary to keep innovative minds for humans.

Living in an information era, we are using lots of technology products which are based on innovations. such as digital camera, laptop, and smart phone. These innovations bring us great convenience, arouse【激发】 our enthusiasm【热情】 for future life, and help our society to move forward. As a matter of fact, intelligent people in large numbers have realized the significance【重要性】 of innovation for our social development: without innovation, industries【产业】 can never be modernized; without innovation, our medical science can never cure so many fatal【致命的】 diseases; and without innovation, there would be no electronic products we use today.

We should bear in mind【牢记】 that innovation does enhance【促进】 the social, scientific and cultural advancement. Thus, students in schools should be educated to think critically【批判性思考】 to foster【培养】 their innovate awareness【意识】; scientists should be supported with financial aids to propose innovative ideas and carry out【开展】 innovative researches. Of course, as a college student, I am convinced that it is of great necessary for students to propose【提出】 innovative approaches to enhance their efficiency of learning.
